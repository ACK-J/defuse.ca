About Defuse Security

Defuse Security is a one-person security research and development group. We develop security-related open-source software. All Defuse Security pages and software are developed and maintained by Taylor Hornby, unless otherwise noted.

You can find Taylor on Twitter and GitHub. His resume is here.

Defuse.ca's Privacy and Usability Policy

  • Assume every request made to this website is logged (including your User-Agent and IP address).
  • I use Piwik for analytics. The data it collects is hosted locally and is not shared or sold. It will also be deleted regularly.
  • You will never need cookies to use this website.
  • I will never put ads on this site no matter how popular it gets.
  • I will refrain from including external content (from other domains) as much as possible.
  • This site will always be functional with JavaScript disabled.
