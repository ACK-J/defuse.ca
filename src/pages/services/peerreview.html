Peer Review & Security Testing

Security is hard. The only way to obtain a higher level of security is through peer review. When many people study a solution to a problem, they can be more sure that it is the correct solution. Nobody can catch all of the mistakes themselves, sometimes you need a third party to look at your ideas. That is what I intend to do. I will review your software, cryptographic protocol, or ideas for security weaknesses.

If your software is going to be released as free (as in freedom) or open source, I will generally review it for free. If your software or idea is closed source, or will be patented, I will charge a small amount for the work that I do.

What You Get

  • Thorough review of your software, protocol, or idea.
  • Report including all possible attack vectors and mitigations of these threats.
  • OSSBox Seal of Approval if we find your software to be secure.
  • Greater level of user trust because a third party is putting their name behind your software.
  • Web page on ossbox.com describing the security features of your product along side our in-depth analysis.
  • If found to be insecure, you will get tips on how to improve your software or idea.

Cost

  • FREE for open source and free (as in freedom) software. (When time permits)
  • $20 per hour for closed source or patented technology. An average review takes about 1 to 2 hours.
  • Free if you are not satisfied.

This is NOT malicious hacking for hire. It is impossible for any one person to find all the security problems in a system. Therefore, I cannot possibly guarantee I will find them ALL problems, but I do try my best.
